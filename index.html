Miles Morales

About Miles

Miles Morales, a teenager who gains spider-like powers and abilities, fighting crime and protecting his city in the Marvel universe as Spider-Man.

Miles Morales
AKA: Spider-Man

Miles' Characteristics

  1. Spider-Based abilities - proportionate strength, speed, stamina, durability & reflexes.
  2. Crawl along surfaces by his fingertips and feet.
  3. Can preceive dangers around him, before seeing or hearing them.
  4. Has the power to direct a bio-electric charge like a sting, including the ability to stun enemies.
  5. Camouflage himself to the point of invisibility.
